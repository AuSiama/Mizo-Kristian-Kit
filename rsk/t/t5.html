Rawngbawl Starter Kit - 05

Ram leh khawtlang rawngbawlna

Thutaka din ngheh leh kan ram tana malsawmna nih dan

Rawngbawlna hi biakin chhungah chauh a tawp lo. Ringtu dik chu kan ram leh khawtlang tan malsawmna ni tura koh kan ni. Isuan Chi leh êng (Salt and Light) ah min ruata. Pathian rorelna dik chu kan awmna hmunah kan thehdarh tur a ni.

Zirtur thupuite

Matthaia 5:13-16 ah Isuan, "Lei chi in ni e" a ti. Chi in chaw a ti tui ang hian ringtute hi khawtlang timawi tu eitlaka (preserve)siam tur kan ni.

  • Khawtlang nun ṭha lo (sual/diklohna) lak aṭangin ṭhatna thlen tu nih.
  • I nun êng hmangin mite'n Pathian an hmuh theih nan entawn tlak nih.
  • Zingkara inkhawm leh hnatlangahte mawhphurhna la ngamtu leh tlawmngai nih.

1 Timothea 2:1-2 ah chuan lalte leh thuneitute tana ṭawngṭaisak turin min ngen a, chu chu Pathian lawmzawng a ni.

  • Roreltuṭhate kan neih theih nan leh rorelna dik a awm theih nan ṭawngṭai tur.
  • Sorkar leh khawtlang hruaitute an tlinlohna sawisel aiin ṭawngṭai pui ṭhin zawk tur.
  • Ram chhungah thlamuanna leh Pathian ṭihna a awm theih nan dilsak tur.

Ram chu a dinngheh theih nan dikna leh thutak a ngai a ni. Ringtute chuan khawtlang sualna te, eirukna te leh hlemhletnate hi kan do tlat tur a ni.

  • Inthlan (Elections) nikhua ah thutak dinpui a, sum leh paia inlei duh loh.
  • Mahni hnathawhna theuhvah rinawmna leh Pathian ṭihna nena hna thawh.
  • Hleihluak taka rorelna (injustice) laka dikna aw (voice) nih ngam.

Rawngbawlna hlawhtling chu midangte tana malsawmna nih hi a ni. Pathianin mi rethei te, hmeithaite leh ṭanpui ngaite duhsak turin min chah ṭhin.

  • Harsatna tawkte, damlo leh chanhai te ṭanpui.
  • Tawngkam mai ni lovin, thiltihin Pathian hmangaihna lantir.
  • Ruihhlo in a tihbuai te leh beidawngte Krista lama hruai.

I hria em? 'Felnain hnam a chawimawi'

Thufingte 14:34 ah chuan, "Felnain hnam a chawimawi a, Sualna erawh chu hnam tin tân hmingchhiatna a ni." a ti a ni. Ram a lo hausak a, a lo ropui theih nan building sang tak tak leh sum leh pai ngawt a tawk lova, mipuite nunchang leh felna (righteousness) hi a pawimawh ber a ni!

Ram leh khawtlang rawngbawlna tehfung

Ram tana ṭawngṭaina leh inpekna 100%
Khawtlanga thutak leh dikna a dinngheh (Integrity) 100%
Mirethei leh chanhai te ṭanpui leh chhawmdawl 100%
