Rawngbawl Starter Kit - 02

Chhungkaw rawngbawlna

In chhungkhur aṭanga Pathian ram zauh ṭan dan

Rawngbawlna hlawhtling chu biakin emaw khawtlang aṭanga ṭan lovin, kan chhungkua, kan in chhung lum aṭanga inṭan tur a ni zawk. Pathianin chhungkua hi kohhran te ber atana a din a ni a, Chhungkaw rawngbawlna a hlawhtlin loh chuan pawn lam rawngbawlna a tlo thei lo.

Zirtur thupuite

In chhungkhurah Pathian rorelna a nghet em tih tehna lian ber pakhat chu Chhungkaw inkhawm (Family Altar) hi a ni. Hei hi chhungkuaa Pathian kan biakna hun a ni.

  • Nitin hun bi tukin chhungkuain Bible chhiar ho leh ṭawngṭai ho na neih ṭhin tur a ni.
  • Fate'n ṭawngṭai dan leh Pathian thu an hmelhriat ṭanna hmun pawimawh ber a ni.
  • Harsatna leh buaina kan tawh te, Pathian hnena thlen hona hmun thlamuanthlak tak a ni.

Nupa inkar ṭhatna leh inhmangaihna hi Pathian rawngbawlna ropui tak a ni. Nupa inhmangaihna leh inzah tawnna hian Krista leh Kohhran inlaichinna a entir tur a ni.

  • Nupa inkarah ngaihdam theihna, dawhtheihna leh inngaihtlawmna a awm tur a ni.
  • Pa in chhungkua hmangaihna nen a ho ang a, Nu in finna leh inpekna in a ṭawiawm ang.
  • Nupa inkar buaina chuan chhungkaw thlarau boruak a tichhe ṭhin tih hriat tur.

Thufingte 22:6-ah, "Naupang chu a kalna âwm kawngah chuan zirtîr ula, A upat hun pawhin a thlah lo vang." a ti. Fate thlarau nun enkawl hi nu leh pa mawhphurhna lian ber a ni.

  • Sunday School leh Kohhran chauh rinchhan lovin, in chhungah Bible thu zirtir tur.
  • Thu ngawta hrilh aiin, nu leh pate nundan (example) hi fate'n an zir nasat ber a ni.
  • An thlarau chhandam a nih theih nan nitin an hming lamin ṭawngṭaisak ṭhin rawh.

Chhungkaw rawngbawlna chu mahni in chhungkhurah chauh a tawp tur a ni lova. Midangte, mikhual leh ṭanpui ngaite tana inhawng taka awm hi Pathian lawmzawng tak rawngbawlna a ni.

  • Rome 12:13 chuan "mi thianghlimte tlâkchhamnaah chhawmdâwl ula; mikhualte lâwmin awm fo ula." tih kan hmu.
  • I inah thlamuanna leh hmangaihna boruak siam la, lo leng lut te'n Pathian an hmuh theih nan.
  • Rawngbawltute leh mi harsa te tana i chhungkua leh i inchhung malsawmna nihtir tum rawh.

I hria em? Josua thutiam

Mipuite chu milem biakna lamah an pên bo mek laiin, hruaitu ropui Josua chuan huaisen takin he thu hi a sawi chhuak a ni: "Kei leh ka chhûngte erawh hi zawngin Lalpa rawng a ni kan bâwl dâwn ni" (Josua 24:15). Pawn lam boruakin engtiang pawhin min bei mahse, kan in chhungkhur erawh Pathian Lalna hmun a ni tlat tur a ni!

Chhungkaw rawngbawlna tehfung

Fate thlarau lama puitling tura buaipui leh ṭawngṭaisakna 100%
