Rawngbawl Starter Kit - 04

Kohhran rawngbawlna

Krista taksa tichak leh sawhnghet tura mawhphurhna

Rawngbawlna dik chu mimal leh chhungkuaah a tawp mai tur a ni lo. Pathianin ringtute chu Kohhran, Krista taksa pakhatah min siam a ni. Kohhran hrisel leh chak nei turin mi tin hian mawhphurhna leh rawngbawl tur kan nei ṭheuh a ni.

Zirtur thupuite

Biakin a inkhawm kim hi rawngbawlna bulṭhut pawimawh tak a ni. Hebrai 10:25-ah chuan "ṭhenkhat tih dân anga kan inkhâwm te bânsan lovin, i infuih tawn zâwk ang u; ni chu lo hnai telh telha in hriatin infuih tawn nasa lehzual sauh rawh u." tih kan hmu.

  • Intihchak tawn leh inhnem tawn turin inkhawm a pawimawh a, mahni chauhvin thlarau lama puitlin a theih loh.
  • I awm ve (presence) ringawt pawh kha midangte tan fuihna a ni thei.
  • Inkhawm ngaihthah hi thlarau lam tluk chhiatna leh hnungtawlh inṭanna a ni fo.

Kohhran mipui te hian Pathian hnen aṭangin Thlarau thilpek leh theihna hrang hrang kan dawng ṭheuh a. Hengte hi Kohhran tichak nan kan hmang ṭangkai tur a ni.

  • Zai thiam, thu sawi thiam, thil siam thiam, rorel thiam emaw mi hnem thiam te pawh a ni thei.
  • Kohhranah "Eng nge ka dawn ang" tih aiin "Eng nge ka pek chhuah ang" tih ngaihtuah zawk tur.
  • Mahni theihna te zep bo lova, chapona tel lova inngaitlawm taka hlan tur a ni.

Kohhran a lo chak a, rawngbawlna a chak theih nan hruaitu leh mipui thawhhona a pawimawh em em a ni.

  • Kohhran hruaitu (Pastor, Upa, rawngbawltu) te hming lamin ṭawngṭaisak ṭhin rawh.
  • An tlinlohna sawisel a phun aiin, an chaklohna laiah dâwm leh puih tum zawk tur.
  • Thurawn ṭha leh ngaihdan siamṭhatna (constructive feedback) hna chu inhmangaihna nen thlen ṭhin tur a ni.

Kohhran in Pathian ram zauh hna a thawh theih nan sum leh pai a pawimawh a. Mahni neih aṭanga rinawm taka Pathian hnena pek let hi rawngbawlna ropui tak a ni.

  • Sawma pakhat (Tithe) chu Pathian ta a ni a, rinawm taka pek hian malsawmna a hringchhuak thin.
  • Thilpek (Offering) hmanga rawngbawlna hrang hrang thawh hi ringtute tih tur a ni.
  • "...Pathian chuan hlim taka petu a ni a hmangaih a ni." (2 Korinth 9:7).

I hria em? 'Ekklesia'

Grik ṭawngin Kohhran chu 'Ekklesia'tih a ni a. A awmzia chu "Biakin in sak (building) emaw pawl (organization)" lam ni lovin, "Khawvel aṭanga Pathian tana koh chhuahte (Assembly/Called out ones)" tihna a ni zawk! Chuvangin, building hi kohhran a ni lova, nangmah kha Kohhran chu i ni!

Kohhran rawngbawlna tehfung

Inkhawm leh inpawlkhawmna ngaipawimawh 100%
Mahni theihna (Talent) kohhran tana hman 100%
Thilpek leh sawma pakhata rinawmna 100%
