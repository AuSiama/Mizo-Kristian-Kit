Rawngbawl Starter Kit - 03

Eizawnna leh hnathawh

Pathian rawngbawlna atana kan hna leh eizawnna hman ṭangkai dan

Kan eizawnna leh nitin hnathawh hi eizawnna mai a ni lova. Pathianin kan theihna leh thiamnate hmanga Amah chawimawi tur leh midangte tana malsawmna ni tura min kohna hmun a ni zawk. Kan hnathawhna hmun chu kan rawngbawlna hmunpui ber a ni!

Zirtur thupuite

Hnathawh hi sual luh hnua Pathian ânchhia a ni lo! Genesis 2:15-ah sual a awm hma hauhvin Pathianin Adama kha huan enkawl turin hna a lo pe tawh a ni.

  • Hnathawh chu thil thianghlim, Pathian nena thilsiam enkawl dun a thawhhona a ni.
  • Hna hniam leh hna sang a awm lova, rinawm taka kan thawh phawt chuan Pathian chawimawina vek a ni.
  • Thatchhiatna hi Pathian duh loh zawng tak a ni a, taimak hi ringtute mizia tur a ni.

Kolossa 3:23 ah chuan, "in tih apiang chu mihring tân anga ti lovin, Lalpa tân anga ti zâwkin, châk takin ti rawh u; Lalpa Krista rawng in bâwl a nih hi" a ti a ni.

  • I boss emaw i thawhpuite hmuh chauhva thawk ṭha (eye-service) ni lovin, Pathian mit hmuhah rinawm rawh.
  • Eirukna (Corruption), hlemhletna leh dawt sawi lakah fihlim tlat tur a ni.
  • Office kai hun, ban hun, leh sum enkawl kawngah Pathian ṭihna nen dik takin nung rawh.

Biakin chhung chauh hi rawngbawlna hmun a ni lo. I office, i dawr, i lo, i school emaw i lirthei khalh lai kha i rawngbawlna hmunpui ber chu a ni.

  • I thawhpuite chungah zaidamna, inngaihtlawmna leh hmangaihna lantir ṭhin rawh.
  • Ngaihtuahna dik lo leh ṭawngkam chhe tak tak lakah Pathian fa i nih zia tarlangin nung rawh.
  • I nundan ṭha tak khan thuhril ropui tak aiin midangte nun a khawih (influence) zawk ṭhin.

Eizawnna hi sum leh pai hailuhna a ni a. Mahse, 1 Timothea 6:10 ah "Tangka sum ngainat hi sual tinrêng bul a ni si a" a ti. Sum hi tangkai takin kan hmang tur tur a ni, kan pathian tur a ni lo.

  • Thlan tui far a thawkchhuak thin tur kan ni a, awlsam taka sum lakluh uar loh tur.
  • I thil thawhchhuah aṭangin Pathian ta (Sawma pakhat) rinawm takin pe ṭhin rawh.
  • I sum hmuh chhuahte chu mi rethei ṭanpui nan leh Pathian ram zauh nan hmang ṭangkai rawh.

I hria em? 'Vocation' awmzia

Eizawnna (Vocation) tih thumal hi Latin ṭawng 'Vocare' aṭanga lo kal a ni a, a awmzia chu 'Kohna (Calling)' tihna a ni. Tichuan, Pastor emaw Missionary te chauh hi 'Pathian koh' an ni lova, i hna, i eizawnna, leh i thiamnate kha, malsawmna ni tura Pathian kohna che a ni!

Eizawnnaa rawngbawlna tehfung

Hna rinawm taka thawh (Integrity & Diligence) 100%
Thawhpuite hnena Krista mizia lantir 100%
Sum lakluh kawnga lungawina leh a hman ṭhat 100%
