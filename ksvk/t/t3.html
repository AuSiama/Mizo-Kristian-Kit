Kristian Survival Kit - 03

Ṭawngṭai dan

Pathian nena inbiakpawhna leh thlarau lam thawk lak dan

Ṭawngṭaina chu thlarau lam thawk lakna a ni a, ringtu nunah chuan thil pawimawh berte zing ami a ni. Engtin nge dik tak leh awmze nei taka Pathian kan biak theih ang? Isuan ṭawngṭai dan a zirtirte a lo kawhhmuh tawh ang khan i zirchiang ang u.

Zirrut thupuite

Ṭawngṭaina chu ngenna (dilna) hlir a ni tur a ni lo. Ṭawngṭai dan awlsam leh famkim tak pakhat chu 'ACTS' model hman hi a ni.

  • A (Adoration): Pathian chawimawina leh fakna a bulṭan tur.
  • C (Confession): Kan sualnate inhriata Pathian hnena thupha chawina.
  • T (Thanksgiving): A thil tih ṭhat leh malsawmna te avanga lawmthu sawina.
  • S (Supplication): Midangte tana dilsakna leh mahni mamawh ngenna.

Pathian chuan ṭawngkam mawi thlap leh thluk nalh aiin thinlung aṭanga rawn chhuak dik tak a ngaihlu zawk.

  • Midangte hmuha ṭha leh mawi tur ringawta ṭawngṭai loh tur (Mathaia 6:5).
  • Thil theihnghilh hlauh vanga thumal sawi nawn sek a ngai lo.
  • Pa in a fa a ngaithla ang hian, i nihna ang tak leh tlang takin i inpuangchhuak dawn nia.

Ṭawngṭaina chu kan duh zawng Pathian tihtir kan tumna a ni lova, Pathian duhzawng kan nuna thlentirna a ni zawk.

  • Isuan Gethsemani huanah "Keima thu ni lovin, nangma thu ni zawk rawh se" a tih ang khan.
  • A thu (Bible)-a a thutiamte nen inmilin kan dil tur a ni.
  • Kan dil ang chiaha chhanna kan hmuh loh pawhin, chhanna ṭha zawk min pe ṭhin tih hriata nghah theih a pawimawh.

1 Thessalonika 5:17 ah chuan "Bâng lovin ṭawngṭai ula" tiin min chah a. Hei hi engtiklai pawha maimit chhinga awm tur tihna a ni lo.

  • Biakin aṭanga kan chhuah ruala tawp nghal mai tur a ni lo.
  • Nitin kan hnathawhna hmun leh kan kalna apiangah Pathian nena inzawm rengna a ni zawk.
  • Eng thil pawh tawk ila, Pathian rawn hmasa chung zel thinlung (attitude of prayer) neih hi a ni.

I hria em? 'Lalpa Ṭawngṭaina'

Isuan 'Lalpa Ṭawngṭaina' (The Lord's Prayer) min pek hi a ngai ngaia chham chhuah ringawt a ni lova. Ṭawngṭai dan outline emaw pattern ṭhatak, Pathian ropuinaa inṭan, ngaihdam dilna, sual laka him dilna, keimahni mamawh dilna atana kan entawn tur min pek a ni.

Ṭawngṭaina hrisel tehfung

Ṭawngṭaina ngenna/dilna (Supplication) ngawt a nih lohna 100%
Pathian thutiam leh duhzawng (Bible) hmanga ṭawngṭai theihna 100%
Mahni duhzawng chauh ngaihtuaha ṭawngṭai hlawhtlinna 10%
