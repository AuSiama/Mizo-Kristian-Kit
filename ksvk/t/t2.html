Kristian Survival Kit - 01

Thlarau indona

Hmuh theih loh khawvela kan buaina leh kan chakna thuruk

Ringtu kan lo nih hian thlamuang taka awm mai tur kan ni lova, indona lian tak maiah sipai anga nung turin koh kan ni zawk. He indona hi mihring leh mihring indona ni lovin, Thlarau indona a ni a. Hnehna chang turin he indona awmzia hi kan hriat a pawimawh hle.

Zirtur thupuite

He indona hi ralthuam hmanga indona, hmuh theihah a thleng lova, kan rilru, ngaihtuahna, leh thlarauvah a thleng ber a ni.

  • Kan ngaihtuahna hi Setanan a beih nasat ber hmun a ni (2 Kor. 10:4-5).
  • Khawvel ngaihdan leh Pathian thu inrem lohna hi indona pakhat chu a ni.
  • Tisa chakna leh Thlarau duhzawng indo hi nitin kan hmachhawn a ni.

Ephesi 6:12 ah kan hmelmate chu tisa leh thisen an nih loh thu chiang takin a sawi a. Hmelma hmuh theih loh lakah kan ding a ni.

  • Diabola leh a tirhkohte (Ramhuai leh thlarau sualte).
  • Khawvel dan (Worldly system) Pathian kalh zawnga kal reng.
  • Kan chhungrila sual chakna (Flesh) awm te hi an ni.

He indonaah hian mahni theihna ngawtin kan chak dawn lova, Pathianin a sipai thuam famkim inbel turin min ti a ni.

  • Thutak kawnghren, felna awmphaw, chanchin tha hrilna pheikhawk, rinna phaw, chhandamna lukhum leh Thlarau ngunhnam te kan inbel tur a ni.
  • Ṭawngṭaina hi ralthuam theh chhuahna pawimawh ber a ni.
  • Isuan kraws-a a hnehna kha kan innghahna chu a ni.

Indona chu a rapthlak hle na a, a tawpah erawh tunge chak dawn tih kan hrechiang tawh a ni.

  • Isua Krista kraws-a a thih khan Setana lu a tithitling tawh a ni.
  • Kolossa 2:15 ah Setana te hnehin Kraws-ah a tarlang tih kan hmu.
  • Kan theihna ni lovin, min hmangaihtu avangin ropui taka ngamtu kan ni Rom 8:37.

I hria em? 'Diabolos'

Grik ṭawnga Diabolos (Diabola/Devil) awmzia chu "Hektu" emaw "Puhtu" tihna a ni. Setana ralthuam hlauhawm ber chu rinhlelhna, dawt leh unau inkara innghirnghona thlen hi a ni fo!
