Saltanna aṭanga zalenna

Sual inphum nghet (Addiction/Habit) aṭanga chhuah zalena awm dan

Kan nunah sual inphum nghet, sim kan duh ngawih ngawih pawha kan theih loh (addiction) a awm ṭhin. Heng saltanna lakah hian theihna mai ni lovin, Pathian hmanrua kan mamawh a ni. Isuan "Fapain a tih zalen che u chuan, in zalen takzet ang" a ti. Engtin nge chu zalenna chu kan chan theih ang?

Zirtur thupuite

Hma kan sawn theih nan kan dinhmun dik tak kan pawm a ngai hmasa ber. "Zep ṭhen nei lovin sual lakah ka tang a ni" tih inpuanchhuah hi zalenna kawngkhar a ni.

  • Mahni insawi thiamna leh midang puhna kalsan a, mawhphurhna lak tur.
  • Pathian hmaah leh mi rintlak (mentor/pastor) hnenah inpuanchhuah ngam tur .
  • Thupbo emaw zep ruk tlatna hian sual chu chakna a pe ṭhin a ni.

Tisa chakna ringawt aiin, chu sual ti tura min nawrtu chhungril na (pain, trauma, ruakna) a awm ṭhin. Chu bulṭhut chu zawn chhuah a ngai.

  • Engtikah nge he thil hi ka tih chakna a zual ṭhin? (Lungngaih laiin, mal ngawih ngawiha in hriat laiin?) tih inen fiah tur.
  • Kan chhungrila ruakna hi Pathian chauh lo chuan a hnawhkhat thei lo.
  • Setanan he kan chaklohna hi hmanrua ah a hmang ṭhin tih hriat chian tur.

Sual hnawl ringawt a tawk lova, thil ṭhaa thlak thleng (replace) a ngai a ni. Tlanchhiatna tur hmunah chuan tlan chhiat mai tur a ni.

  • Sual lama min hruai theitu thlemna lakah daidanna (boundaries) khauh tak siam tur.
  • Thil ṭhalo tih chakna a lo luhin, thil dang (ṭawngṭai, exercise, hla ngaihthlak) hmanga thlakthleng tur.
  • Fimkhurna hi hlauhna a ni lova, finna a ni zawk Thufingte 22:3.

Johana 15-ah Isuan, "Keimahah awm reng rawh u... keimah lovin eng mah in ti thei si lo" a ti a. Mahni chakna ngawtin saltanna (addiction) a hneh theih loh.

  • Nitin Bible chhiar leh ṭawngṭaina hmanga thlarau lam tihchak a ngai.
  • Hnehna chu vawilehkhata lo thleng vek nghal ni lovin, nitina hnehna hmuh chhoh zel (process) a ni thei tih pawm tur.
  • I tluk palh pawhin, indawm kun lovin Isua hnenah kir leh nghal zel rawh.

I hria em? 'Neuroplasticity' leh Pathian Thu

Science chuan kan thluak hi ngaihtuahna thar hmangin a zia a inthlak (rewire) thei tih a hmuchhuak a, chu chu 'Neuroplasticity' an ti. Hei hi Bible-in Rom 12:2-a "in rilru a thara awmin lo danglam zâwk tawh rawh u." a tih nen hian a inmil chiah a ni! Pathian thu a thluak chawm reng hian zawi zawiin kan sual chakna hlui a ti reh ṭhin.
