Thlarau Thianghlim

Kan chhungah a awm tih hriatna leh fiahna

Isua Krista kan rin rualin, mahni ngawta khawvel hmachhawn turin min kalsan lova. Ama aiawh tur leh min awmpui reng turin Thlarau Thianghlim min pe a ni. Kan chhungrila Pathian Nung a cheng a ni tih chu engtin nge kan hriat theih ang? I lo zirchiang ang u.

Zirtur thupuite

Rom 8:16 ah chuan "Thlarau ngei chuan, keimahni thlarau nên Pathian fate kan ni tih min hriattîr ṭhîn" tih kan hmu. Hei hi thil mak emaw feeling satliah nilovin, chhungril hriatna thuk tak a ni.

  • Thilsual kan tih palh in hrehawm tihna leh sim duhna min nehtir (conviction) ṭhin.
  • Pathian hnaih chakna leh biak chakna thinlung min pe ṭhin.
  • Harsatna leh rinhlelhna karah pawh, chhandam kan ni tih thlamuanna min pe ṭhin a ni.

Mahni theihna a kan hneh theih loh sual thil te hi, Pathian puihna rinchhana kan hneh theih hian kan chhungah Thlarau Thianghlim a thawk tih a chiang a ni.

  • Tisa chakna sual min dopui a, nun thianghlim min neihtir ṭhin.
  • Nunhlui ṭhalo (addiction/habit) kalsan turin theihna leh chakna min pe ṭhin.
  • Setana thlemna hmachhawn turin huaisenna leh Pathian thu hriatrengna min pe ṭhin.

Thingkung chu a rah ah a hriat theih ang hian, Thlarau Thianghlim hnathawh rahchhuah chu Galatia 5:22-23 a kan hmuh Thlarau rah aṭang hian a fiah ṭhin.

  • Kan mizia pangngai nilo, mize ṭha zawk (hmangaihna, dawhtheihna, inngaihtlawmna) kan lo neih ṭan hian Thlarauvin hna a thawk tihna a ni.
  • Mi dangte chunga ngilneihna leh zaidamna a lo piang ṭhin.
  • Harsatna leh buaina karah pawh lawmna (joy), chhungrila hrihfiah theih si loh a awm tlat ṭhin.

Thlarau Thianghlim chu "Thutak Thlarau" tih a ni a. Bible hi lehkhabu satliah anga chhiar liam mai lovin, a nungin min hriatthiamtir ṭhin a ni.

  • Bible chang kan chhiar in kan nunah a rawn nung chhuak (Rhema) ṭhin.
  • Pathian duhzawng leh a lungawina tur thlan thiamna (discernment) min pe ṭhin.
  • Thutak kawngah min hruai lutin, zirtirna diklo lakah min veng ṭhin a ni.

I hria em? 'Parakletos'

Grik ṭawngin Thlarau Thianghlim hi 'Parakletos' tia koh a ni a. A awmzia chu "Kan kianga awm tura koh, Thlamuantu, Ṭanpuitu, leh min Sawi-sak-tu (Advocate)" tihna a ni. Khawvelah hian mal takin i awm ngai lo, Engkimtitheia chu i chhungah a cheng reng a ni!
