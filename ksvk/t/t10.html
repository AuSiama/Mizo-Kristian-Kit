Kristian Survival Kit - 10

Ringtu zinkawng chhunzawm zel dan

Thlarau lama puitlinna kawng leh rinawm taka hringnun hman dan

Rinawmna chu bul ṭan ṭhat ringawt ni lovin, a tawp thlenga ṭhat hi a ni. Thlarau lam zinkawngah hian chawlh hman a ni lova, Krista kan hmuh hma chu theihtawpa kan tlan a ngai a ni. Engtin nge he zinkawngah hian chau lova kan tlan reng theih ang?

Zirtur thupuite

Hebrai 12:1-ah "...kan hmaa intlânsiakna tûr awmah hian chhel takin i tlân zêl ang u." a ti. Kristian nun hi 100-meter dash a ni lova, marathon a ni zawk.

  • I nunah rit phurh leh sual awlsam taka uap ṭhintu che chu dahṭha ngam rawh.
  • Tluk palh chang pawhin, beidawng a tlu hlen mai lovin, Isua hmingin tho leh nghal zel rawh.
  • Nitin hian i tum ram (Heaven) mitthla tlat la, chu chuan chakna a pe ang che.

Zinkawngah hian rilru la peng theitu (distractions) a tam em em a. Kan rinna bul ṭantu leh tifamkimtu Isua chauh kan en a ngai a ni.

  • Mi dangte tlukna leh thatlohna ah mit fuk tir suh; i thlirtur chu Isua chauh a ni.
  • Khawvel nawmsakna leh sum leh pai te hian Pathian aṭangin i mit an la peng tur a ni lo.
  • Petera'n tui chunga a kal laia tuifawn a en a, a pil ang khan, buaina aiin Isua en tlat rawh.

Zinkawnga tlu lo tur chuan thlarau lam chaw ṭha nitina kan ei a ngai. Feeling nei lo chung pawha thil ṭha tih luihna (Discipline) kan mamawh.

  • Chak loh chang pawhin, nitin Bible chhiar leh ṭawngṭai hi tih ṭhan (habit) ah neih tlat rawh.
  • Kohhrana inkhawm leh inpawlkhawmna tlansan ngai suh.
  • Rawngbawlnaah inhmang la, midangte tan malsawmna nih tum zel rawh.

Tirhkoh Paula'n a nuna a tlanchhuah dawn khan, "Intihsiakna ṭha chu ka bei zo tawh a, ka tlânsiakna kawng ka hlen tawh a, rinna chu ka vawng reng tawh a ni" a ti a ni.

  • He khawvela kan tuarna leh hrehawmna te hi nakina lallukhum kan chan tur nen chuan tehkhin rual a ni lo.
  • Isuan "Bawih ṭha leh rinawm, i pu lawmnaah lut rawh" a tih hun tur che chu nghakhlel hle rawh.
  • Tun aṭang hian i zinkawng tawpna tur thlir lawk thiam la, rinawm takin pen zel rawh.

I hria em? 'Sanctification' process

Pathian hnena thiamchantir (Justification) chu vawikhat thleng a ni a. Mahse, nitina Krista mizia kan an chhoh zelna hi 'Sanctification' (Tihthianghlimna) a ni, he thil hi chu zinkawng thui tak, kan damchhung zawnga thleng (process) a ni. Chuvangin, i damchhungin thlarau lama ṭhan i ngai tih hria ang che!

Zinkawng puitlin tehfung

Feeling leh phur laia tlan theihna 25%
Thlarau lam discipline vawn leh chhel taka tlanna 80%
Isua chauh ena a tawp thlenga rinawmna 100%
