Kristian Starter Kit - 03

Isuan min tlanna avanga Pathian nena inzawm lehna

Sual daidanna ṭhiata fa nihna min pek kir leh dan

Adama tlukna avanga Pathian nena kan inkar chhia leh thihna chu, Isua Krista, kraws-a a thihna leh a thawhlehna hmangin siamṭhat a ni ta. Hei hi Tlanna kan tih chu a ni a, he mi avang hian Pathian nen inlaichinna thar leh nung kan nei leh thei ta a ni.

Zirtur thupuite

Sual man chu thihna a ni a, chu hremna chu kan pumpelh theih loh a ni. Mahse, Pathianin min hmangaih em avangin A Fapa mal neih chhun, Isua Krista chu khawvelah a rawn tir a. Isuan kan aiawhin kraws-ah a tuar a, kan sual man min pek sak ta a ni.

  • Isua chu kan aiawhtu (Substitute) a ni a, kan tuar tur min tuarsak a ni.
  • Kraws hian Pathian hmangaihna leh A rorelna dik (Justice) a tarlang kawp a ni.
  • Isua Krista chuan Kraws hmangin Pathian leh mihring inkar chhe tawh chu a rem ta a ni.

Isua kraws-a a thih rual khan, Biakbuk-a Hmun Thianghlim leh Hmun Thianghlim Ber daidangtu Puanzar chu a thler a. Hei hian awmze thuk tak a nei.

  • Puithiam kal tlang ngai lovin, mimal tinin Pathian kan hnaih thei ta.
  • Sualin min daidang tawh lova, huaisen takin Pathian lalthutphah kan pan thei ta.
  • Pathian awmpuina chu hmun pakhat Biakbuk-ah chauh ni tawh lovin, khawi hmunah pawh a awm ta.

Isua rin avanga kan chan hlu ber pakhat chu 'Thiamchantirna' hi a ni. Hei hi dan lam (legal term) ṭawngkam a ni a, roreltu (Pathian) in kan sualte min ngaidam a, Isua felna (righteousness) chu keimahni felna anga min pawm sak na hi a ni.

  • Sual nei ngai miah lo (just-as-if-I'd-never-sinned) ang maia Pathianin min thlirna a ni.
  • Kan thiltih ṭhat avanga hlawhchhuah ni lovin, Isua thiltih rinna avanga thilthlawnpek a ni.
  • Thiamchantir kan nih avangin Pathian thinurna lakah kan him ta.

Sual bawih aṭanga tlan chhuah kan nih hnuah, Pathianin min chhuah zalen ringawt lo min lova, Ama chhungkuaah min la lut a, A fa atan min pawm (adopted) ta a ni. Hei hi inlaichinna thuk ber chu a ni.

  • Pathian chu hlauh tur leh hlauhtlawn tur ni lovin, "Abba, Pa" tiin kan ko thei ta.
  • Pathian fa kan nih avangin, A rochun te kan lo ni ve ta.
  • Khawvelah hian fahrah kan ni lova, Pa hmangaihtu leh min ngaihsaktu kan nei.

I hria em? 'Tetelestai'

Isuan kraws-a a thih dawn a, "A kin ta" a tih khan, Grik ṭawngin 'Tetelestai' a ti a ni. He thumal hi hmanlai hun chuan sumdawnna hmanrua a ni a, lehkha phek-ah "Ba zawng zawng pek tlak vek a ni" (Paid in full) tih entir nan an ziak ṭhin. Kan sual man chu Isuan a taksa hlanin a pe tlak vek tawh!
