Chhandam nih inhriatna (Assurance)

Pathian thutiam ngheta innghahna leh thlamuanna

Ringtute hian chhandam kan ni tih hi kan hrechiang thei em? Aw, thei e! Kan chhandamna hi kan rilru puthmang (emotions) mai maiah innghat lovin, Pathian thutiam rinawm leh dikah a innghat zawk a ni. He inhriatfiahna ah hian kan himzia i lo zir ang u.

Zirtur thupuite

Chhandam nih inhriatna (Assurance) hi kan rilrua thil thleng (feelings) mai ni lovin, Pathianin A thu-a a sawi (promise) a ni. 1 Johana 5:13 ah chuan "Chatuana nunna in nei tih in inhriat theih nân, nangni Pathian Fapa hming ringtute hnênah hêng thu hi ka ziak a ni." a ti.

  • Mihring feel-na hi chu a inthlak danglam reng a, a rintlak loh.
  • Pathian Thu (Bible) erawh chu a nghet reng a, a danglam ve ngai lo.
  • Kan innghahna tur chu 'Ka inngaihdan' ni lovin, 'Pathian sawidan' a ni zawk.

Rom 8:16 ah chuan "Thlarau ngei chuan, keimahni thlarau nên Pathian fate kan ni tih min hriattîr ṭhîn" tiin kan hmu. Kan chhungrilah hian Pathian fa kan nihzia min hriattirtu Thlarau Thianghlim a awm a ni.

  • Sual kan tih hian nuamlo min tihtir tawh zawka, sim turin Thlarau Thianghlim chuan min ngen ṭhin (Hei hi Pathian fa kan nih fiahna a ni).
  • Isua Krista chawimawi leh hnaih chakna min pe ṭhin.
  • Pathian chu hlauhawm ni lovin "Abba, Pa" tia ko ngam turin huaisenna min pe a ni.

Isuan Johana 10:28 ah chuan "an hnênah chatuana nunna ka pe a; kumkhuain an boral lo vang a, tu mân ka kut ata mi chhuhsak hek lo vang." tiin a sawi. Kan himna Krista hi a ropui hle a ni!

  • Kan chhandamna hi kan kutah ni lovin, Krista kutah a awm zawk a ni.
  • Kan chak lohnaah leh kan tluk palh pawhin Krista kut chuan min vuan nghet reng.
  • Kan felna (human efforts) ni lovin, Krista felna leh thiltihah a innghat tlat a ni.

Chhandam kan nih chianna (evidence) pakhat chu kan nuna inthlakthlengna awm hi a ni. 1 Johana 2:3 ah chuan, "Tin, a thupêkte pawm kan nih chuan, chumiah chuan amah kan hria tih kan inhria e." a ti a ni.

  • Sual nuam ti ṭhin khan sual kan haw ta a, Pathian thu awih kan chak ta zawk ṭhin.
  • Ringtute (Pathian fate) kan hmangaih a, an zinga awm nuam kan ti ṭhin.
  • Piantharna dik tak chuan rah ṭha a chhuah ngei ngei ṭhin a ni.

I hria em? Assurance leh Presumption

Assurance (Inhriatchianna) leh Presumption (Ngaihdan mai maia innghah) hi a inang lo hle. Assurance chu inngaitlawm taka Pathian thutiam chunga innghahna a ni a. Presumption erawh chu Pathian thu pawisa lova, sual kawnga kal reng chunga mahni duhthu-a 'Chhandam ka ni!' inti ve ngawt hi a ni.

Chhandamna nih inhriatna dik

Pathian Thutiam (Bible) a innghah him dan 100%
Mahni theihna leh feel-naa innghah rintlak dan 0%
Isua kuta kan himna leh humhimna 100%
